Community Service

Sea Aggie Coding Club - Event Coordinator

As the Events Coordinator, I play a key role in shaping the experiences our members gain throughout the year. By preparing, planning, and executing a variety of events, I aim to create opportunities that not only foster learning but also inspire a deeper passion for Computer Science. We've hosted events introducing members to programming languages like Python, which give them hands-on coding experience, as well as inviting Guest Lecturers from across campus who bring unique perspectives on topics ranging from data science to cybersecurity.

Additionally, I have organized Hackathons, which provide a collaborative and competitive environment for students to solve real-world problems and push the boundaries of their skills. My ultimate goal with this organization is to empower members to build strong portfolios that showcase their capabilities while also broadening their understanding of just how varied and impactful Computer Science applications can be. From software development and artificial intelligence to embedded systems and web technologies, I aim to provide a well-rounded experience that prepares our members for their future careers. Link

TAMU Big Event

Participating in The Big Event has been an incredible experience for me. As the largest one-day, student-run service project in the nation, it brings together tens of thousands of Texas A&M students each spring to give back to the residents of Bryan and College Station. For over 42 years, Aggie students have taken part in this tradition, completing service projects like yard work, window washing, and painting for local residents. I felt truly honored to be a part of this event, giving back to the university and community that have given me so much. It was a meaningful way to express my gratitude and contribute to the community that supports us all. Link
